Trout Adventure

Moderate Currents

Need a bit more of a challenge? The Trout Adventure is a great place to start! Class III whitewater rafting requires more awareness and maneuvering as you paddle through larger, faster currents and higher waves. Brief reprieves between active sections of the river will give you a short rest before tackling the demanding water again. Anyone above the age of 10 is allowed to join on this fun filled adventure down the rapids.

    Salmon Adventure

    Fast Currents

    Time to test your limits. The Salmon Adventure is no walk in the park, harsh currents and high waves await you in this adventure. You’ll get the workout of a lifetime as you push your body to the limit and try to keep up with the river. Class IV rapids are fast, long and difficult as you face unavoidable waves and constricted routes to maneuver through. You and your team will be accompanied by an experienced and trustworthy guide as you tackle this challenge. Are you ready to take on the Salmon Adventure?
